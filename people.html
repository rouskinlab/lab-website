People

The people behind the measurements and the models.

The lab brings together RNA biology, virology, biochemistry, statistical inference, machine learning, and method development. Below: current members at HMS, and the alumni network now spread across academia and industry.

Principal investigator

Silvi Rouskin

Assistant Professor of Microbiology at Harvard Medical School. The lab develops RNA structure measurements and the statistical and computational methods needed to read them.

Principal investigator

Silvi Rouskin, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor, Department of Microbiology · Harvard Medical School

Silvi's lab studies how RNA folds in living cells, how the same sequence can adopt multiple structures, and how those alternatives shape viral biology, splicing, and disease.
